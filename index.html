Hello! I am doing reorganization of my place.

Meanwhile you can find a slide show with several inspirational resources that reflected me in one way or another.
Probably they could reflect the way you are doing things too.

1. When You End Up Being Part Of A System Be A Linchpin

It could happen that you are part of a system working from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Don't complain about that and don't be mediocre in anything you do.
Remember that the system is not as important as you. Be not easily replaceable. Be a Linchpin

2. Or Just Completely Reinvent Yourself

Know that now you have more chances than ever to make a difference.
Seth Godin will teach you how to reinvent yourself with
showing you how to connect, be generous, make art, ship, fail, and learn.

3. Know That It Is Better To Create Things Led By A Principle

Create things but preferably towards solving particular problem.
Don't worry even if it is only in your head. Just invent your solution lead by a guiding principle.
Bret Victor explains how to do it...

4. The Fear Of Failure Will Be Your Last Barrier

Sometimes you have all ingredients to do something great but the fear of failure is your stopper.
Don't be surprised about that. It is valid for anyone.
Learn how to "Poke The Box" and you will be willing to take more risks.

5. Focus And Start...

The world is full of distractions now.
If you cannot focus you will be in trouble.
Learn some strategies how to do it with Leo Babauta.

6. ... Execute On Inspiration ...

Shipping is the outcome of the creative process.
There is one single secret about shipping.
Josh Long and Drew Willson reveal this secret.

7. ... Keep Your Creativity Up ...

We have a lot of inspirational resources to consume from.
However their authors tell these stories when knowing their outcome.
Scott Berkun will help you deal with the terrifying uncertainty of making new things.

8. ... Energize Yourself Daily ...

Creativity requires energy. How to obtain more energy?
Some of the advices from the Altucher's daily practice for your physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual shape could do the trick.

9. ... and Never Ever Stop Playing.

Don't take yourself and what you are doing too seriously.
Play will help you in being successfull.
Play manifesto by Steve Keil still need to be applied on many places not only in Bulgaria
