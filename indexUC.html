Hi! This is a place where I am blogging occasionally in different areas.
My purpose is to keep it structured in way that is more usefull for the reader.

Often we need inspiration sources to keep our learning speed and to be successful in what we are doing
The slide show bellow lists such sources that reflected me in one way or another.
Probably it could reflect the way you are doing things too.

1. Focus.

If you cannot focus it does not matter how smart are you - you will be in trouble.
Learn how to focus in a world full of distractions with Leo Babauta.

2. Then Reinvent Yourself.

Know that now you have more chance than ever to make a difference.
Seth Godin will teach you how to connect, be generous, make art, ship, fail, and learn.

3. Energize Yourself Daily And Be Proactive.

If you are proactive sooner or later the good things will happen.
Proactivity requires energy. How to obtain more energy?
Altucher's daily practice for your physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual shape could do the trick.

4. Create things lead by a principle.

Create things but preferably towards solving particular problem (even if it is only in your head).
Invent your solution lead by a guiding principle.
See example how to do this in the amazing talk by Bret Victor.

5. Don't be afraid of failure.

Sometimes you have all ingredients to do something great but the fear of failure is your stopper.
Check Seth Godin's "Poke The Box" and you will probably stop doing it anymore.

6. The system is not as important as your contribution to it.

Dream jobs are most often created than found.
So probably you will be part of a system working from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Don't complain about that and don't be mediocre in anything you do.
Be not easily replaceable. Be a Linchpin
